# 이 사이트 안드로이드 보안 관련 핵심 내용을 요약 및 구조화해서 위키로 만들어 줘 checklist

## Checklist
- [ ] Determine your current verification status: Check if you distribute exclusively outside of Google Play. If you already use the Google Play Console, review the guides to confirm you meet the existing verification requirements .
- [ ] Identify your developer type: Determine if you need a standard commercial account or the specialized student/hobbyist account for the new Android Developer Console .
- [ ] Sign up for Early Access (October 2025): Register for early access to get an invitation to the exclusive community forum, receive priority support, and test the new Android Developer Console .
- [ ] Complete Verification (March 2026): Create your profile in the new Android Developer Console and verify your identity once the platform opens to all developers .
- [ ] Prepare for Regional Deadlines (September 2026): Ensure your apps are fully verified before September 2026 if you have users in Brazil, Indonesia, Singapore, or Thailand, as unverified apps will be blocked from installation on certified devices in these regions .
